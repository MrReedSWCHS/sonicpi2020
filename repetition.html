4 Repetition

4.1 Looping

You can loop a block of code like this.

loop do
  play 70
  sleep 1
end

The do and end act like brackets. They enclose code blocks in many situations.

4.2 Options

Use amp: to change the volume (amp: 1 is default)

play :cs5, amp: 1.5

4.3 x.times

3.times do
  play 70
  sleep 1
end

You can nest do/end blocks:

3.times do
  play 70
  sleep 1
  
  5.times do
    play 71
    sleep 1
  end
end

Notice there are two ends because there are two dos, like pairs of brackets.

4.4 loop
